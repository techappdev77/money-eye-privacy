Money Eye - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

Thank you for choosing Money Eye. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Financial Data: All your expense records, categories, budgets, and savings goals are stored exclusively on your device and are never transmitted to our servers.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information:

  • Usage Data: Anonymous app interaction data may be collected through Google Play Console for crash reporting and performance monitoring only
  • Device Information: Device type, Android version, and language settings for compatibility purposes

1.3 Third-Party API Data:

  • When using currency conversion features, we send currency codes to exchange rate APIs
  • No personal identifiers are included in these requests

2. Data Storage and Security

All your financial data is stored locally on your device using SQLite database. We do not operate any servers that store your personal financial information.

3. Permissions

  • Internet Access: Used solely for fetching currency exchange rates from public APIs
  • Storage Access: Used only for backup creation and data export features initiated by you

4. Third-Party Services

Exchange Rate APIs: We use public currency conversion services. These services may collect standard log data (IP address, request timestamp) as per their own privacy policies.

Google Play Services: Our app is distributed via Google Play Store, which may collect standard installation and update information.

5. Your Rights

  • Data Export: Export all data to CSV/JSON format via app functionality
  • Data Deletion: Use "Reset All Data" feature in Settings to remove all local data
  • Backup: Create local backups on your device storage

6. Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
